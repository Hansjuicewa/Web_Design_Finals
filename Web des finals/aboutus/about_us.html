  • History of Lato-Lato

Popular as it is in Southeast Asia, it looks like lato-lato originated from the U.S. in the late 1960s. It was called Newton's Yo-Yo and was meant to be an educational toy (Read: physics). The earliest iterations had balls made with tempered glass instead of plastic, which would cause the toy to shatter into tiny pieces during play. This led the American Consumer Product Safety Commission to step in and set safety regulations in place. Some schools decided to ban it altogether..
